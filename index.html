Problem

Every year the number of people who are sorting waste increases slightly, which we can see in decreasing amount of municipal solid waste and growing amount of sorted waste. People are mostly sorting paper, plastics and glass. Collected biowaste comes mostly from gardens in adjacent villages. In residential areas with block of flats, sorted biowaste is insignificant and in some places waste containers are completely missing. It is a commonplace that people throw into biowaste container something that does not belong there, and owing to this, the waste is not able to be recycled. Moreover, there is often a low number of containers for edible fats, oils and textile available in towns.

Therefore, near blocks of flats people create illegal waste dumps, while they still think that they sort waste in that way. It is necessary to sort waste correctly to ensure it can be recycled and amount of other municipal solid waste can decrease even more. We are convinced that the app for right waste sorting may be a solution of the waste sorting problem in a community.

REDIVIO

The core of the app is a map with locations of waste containers . And our goal? Make waste sorting easier. How does REDIVIO work? After installing an app, you will register with a username and password. After login or registration, the app with recycling symbols will open.

You will choose type of waste, for example plastic, and REDIVIO will automatically find the route to the nearest container for plastics. The app is also giving you points . After clicking on the “statistics” icon, the app provides the user with information about collected points.
